Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

DO’s: - Think before posting anything 
- Keep your profiles professional(especially LinkedIn) 
- Verify information before sharing 
- Respectothers’ opinions and privacy 
- Use strong passwords and enable 2FA 
-Share meaningful and positive content 
- Credit original creators whenusing their content

DON’Ts: - Don’t share personal info (OTP, address, passwords) 
- Don’tpost offensive, abusive, or controversial content 
- Don’t spread fakenews or unverified info 
- Don’t overshare your daily life publicly 
-Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling 
- Don’t use weak orrepeated passwords 
- Don’t blindly trust links, DMs, or unknown accounts
